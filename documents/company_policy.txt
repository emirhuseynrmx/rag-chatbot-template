DATAFLOW ANALYTICS — COMPANY POLICY & FAQ
Version 2.1 | Effective June 2025
=========================================

ABOUT US
--------
DataFlow Analytics is a data engineering and machine learning consultancy. We build
data pipelines, predictive models, automation systems, and reporting dashboards for
small-to-medium businesses. Projects are delivered remotely.

Contact: contact@dataflow-analytics.io
Support: support@dataflow-analytics.io
Hours:   Monday – Friday, 09:00 – 18:00 CET


SERVICES AND PRICING
--------------------
All pricing is in EUR and excludes VAT where applicable.

  Starter Package (€1,500 – €3,000)
    - One-time data pipeline or cleaning script
    - Single dashboard or PDF report
    - Delivery within 5 business days
    - Includes one revision round
    - Best for: one-off CSV cleaning, simple automation, basic reporting

  Growth Package (€3,000 – €8,000)
    - Full ETL pipeline with monitoring and alerts
    - Machine learning model (classification or regression) with explanation report
    - REST API endpoint or Telegram bot delivery
    - Delivery within 10-15 business days
    - Includes two revision rounds and 30-day post-launch support
    - Best for: churn prediction, lead scoring, pricing optimization

  Enterprise Package (€8,000+)
    - End-to-end data platform: ingestion, storage, modelling, deployment
    - Custom ML pipeline with SHAP explainability and drift monitoring
    - Team onboarding and knowledge transfer sessions
    - Ongoing retainer option (€1,200/month)
    - Best for: companies needing a dedicated data function without hiring full-time

  Hourly Consulting (€95/hour)
    - Code review, architecture advice, debugging sessions
    - Minimum booking: 2 hours
    - Billed in 30-minute increments thereafter

Custom quotes are provided within 24 hours. Contact us with your dataset size,
integration requirements, and expected output format.


REFUND POLICY
-------------
We offer a satisfaction guarantee on all fixed-scope deliverables.

  - Within 7 days of delivery: Full refund if the deliverable does not match
    the agreed specification in the signed statement of work.
  - Days 8–14: 50% refund if a material defect is identified.
  - After 14 days: No refund, but we will address critical bugs at no charge
    for 30 days post-delivery.

Refund requests must be submitted in writing to support@dataflow-analytics.io
with the project reference number. Refunds are processed within 5 business days
via the original payment method (bank transfer or Stripe).

Hourly consulting sessions are non-refundable once completed.


SUPPORT POLICY
--------------
  - Standard Support: included with all packages; responses within 24 business hours.
  - Priority Support: available for Enterprise clients and retainer subscribers;
    responses within 4 business hours.
  - Critical bugs (pipeline down, data loss): response within 2 hours on business days.
  - Post-project bug fixes: covered for 30 days after final delivery at no cost.
  - Feature additions after delivery are scoped and quoted separately.

To open a support ticket, email support@dataflow-analytics.io with:
  1. Your project reference number (format: DF-YYYY-NNNN)
  2. Description of the issue and steps to reproduce
  3. Any relevant error messages or screenshots


DATA EXPORTS AND FORMATS
------------------------
Deliverables can be provided in the following formats depending on project type:

  Tabular data:    CSV, Excel (XLSX), Parquet, JSON Lines
  Reports:         PDF (A4), HTML dashboard, Jupyter notebook
  APIs:            REST (JSON), webhook (JSONL payloads)
  Databases:       PostgreSQL dump, SQLite file, DuckDB
  Models:          Pickle, ONNX, Joblib, Hugging Face Hub upload

Default format is CSV + PDF report unless otherwise specified. Format changes
after delivery are included in the 30-day support window.


PAYMENT TERMS
-------------
  - Fixed-scope projects: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery.
  - Retainer packages: monthly in advance on the 1st of each month.
  - Hourly sessions: invoiced weekly; due within 7 days.
  - Payment methods: SEPA bank transfer, Stripe (Visa/Mastercard/AMEX), Wise.
  - Late payment (>14 days): 1.5% monthly interest applied automatically.

Invoices are issued in EUR. Currency conversion at Stripe's daily rate for
non-EUR payments.


CONFIDENTIALITY
---------------
All client data is treated as strictly confidential. We:
  - Process data only for the purpose of the agreed project.
  - Do not share client data with third parties.
  - Delete all client data within 30 days of project completion unless
    a longer retention period is agreed in writing.
  - Sign an NDA upon request at no cost.

Data is stored on encrypted EU-region servers (Hetzner Cloud, Frankfurt).


INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
---------------------
  - Code and models developed for a client become the client's property upon
    full payment of the final invoice.
  - We retain the right to describe the project type (not data or code) in our
    portfolio with client approval.
  - Open-source libraries used remain under their respective licenses.


CANCELLATION
------------
  - Before work starts: full refund of the upfront payment.
  - After kick-off but before 50% completion: 50% of the upfront payment is retained.
  - After 50% completion: full upfront payment is retained; no further charges.

Cancellation must be sent in writing to contact@dataflow-analytics.io.


FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
--------------------------

Q: How long does a typical project take?
A: Starter projects: 3-5 business days. Growth projects: 10-15 business days.
   Enterprise projects: 4-12 weeks depending on scope. Timelines are confirmed
   in the statement of work before work begins.

Q: Do you work with non-technical clients?
A: Yes. We handle all technical aspects. You provide your data and business context;
   we handle the engineering, modelling, and reporting.

Q: What data formats do you accept as input?
A: CSV, Excel, JSON, Parquet, PostgreSQL, MySQL, Google Sheets, and REST API endpoints.
   If your data is in a different format, contact us and we will evaluate compatibility.

Q: Can you integrate with our existing tools?
A: We commonly integrate with Zapier, n8n, Google Workspace, Notion, Airtable,
   Telegram, Slack, and major CRMs (HubSpot, Pipedrive, Salesforce).

Q: Do you offer ongoing maintenance?
A: Yes. Retainer packages (€1,200/month) include monitoring, monthly model retraining,
   dashboard updates, and up to 10 hours of development per month.

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes. All data is stored on EU-region encrypted servers. We sign NDAs on request
   and delete all client data within 30 days of project completion.

Q: Can I get a free consultation before committing?
A: Yes. A 30-minute discovery call is free and non-binding. Book via
   contact@dataflow-analytics.io with a brief description of your project.

Q: What if the model accuracy is not good enough?
A: We define accuracy targets and evaluation metrics in the statement of work.
   If the delivered model does not meet the agreed benchmark on your holdout set,
   we iterate until it does at no extra charge within the 30-day support window.

Q: Do you provide the code or only the output?
A: By default we deliver both the production code and the output (report, model, API).
   Code comes with documentation and a brief handover session.

Q: Can you train our team?
A: Yes. We offer 2-hour onboarding sessions (€190) and structured training workshops
   (€450/day) covering Python data stacks, ML pipelines, or dashboard tools.
